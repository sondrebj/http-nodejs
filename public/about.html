What tools do we offer?

At our workshop, we offer a wide range of tools to meet the needs of all users. Whether you're looking for cutting-edge electric tools like our 3D printer and laser cutter, or more traditional tools like a saw, we've got you covered. Our collection of tools is designed to provide students with everything they need to bring their ideas to life, from simple DIY projects to complex builds.

We're proud to offer a comprehensive collection of 50 tools for our students to use. However, it's important to note that some of these tools require users to have a certificate to operate them safely. We prioritize safety at our workshop, so we ask that all students familiarize themselves with our safety protocols and obtain the necessary certificates before using these tools.

Rules for using the Makerspace!

  • Users of the Makerspace are responsible for their own health and safety.
  • Equipment should be handled carefully.
  • Clean up after yourself and put the equipment back in its place.
  • If any equipment is broken, missing, or damaged, users should inform Ida Parelius.
  • Do not use equipment that requires a course without taking the course.
  • Do not take equipment out of the Makerspace.
  • Ask for help if you are unsure or have any questions.
  • No food or drinks on the electronics tables or in the printing room.
